One of Gateshead's finest kept secrets, housed in a Grade II listed former bank building at the heart of Low Fell.

We offer a decadent escape for those in the know — good food, good drinks, good company, and most of all, a good time. From small plates and Sunday roasts to hand-crafted cocktails and fine wine.
